Biography

Born to be a Musician

Born to be a musician, Paresh Shah, started his career in 1995 as a keyboard player who turned out to be a prodigy music composer. He has done brilliant compositions for a wide range of films as well as songs. Technically sound and up to date, he takes you ahead of time.

For him music is a bridge that connects the audience with the film or any form of audio visual. He has established a state of the art recording studio equipped with latest recording facilities. Driven by his vision, he believes in composing distinct and international content.

Over the years, Paresh has composed music for over 200 projects spanning television serials, feature films, advertisements, and live events. His compositions are known for their emotional depth, rich orchestration, and the seamless fusion of traditional Indian instruments with modern production techniques.

Operating from his studio, Paresh Music Studio in Andheri West, Mumbai, he continues to mentor young talent, experiment with new sounds, and create music that resonates across generations. His work has earned him recognition at prestigious platforms including the Indian Telly Awards and the BIG Annual Academy Awards.
